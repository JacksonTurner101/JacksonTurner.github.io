Top Down Scrolling shooter

This game is about a spaceship flying through space destroying asteroids and aliens. This was a personal project that I wanted to create. The gameplay consists of the player trying to reach the end of the level with the highest score by not crashing into the asteroids and aliens, and destroying the asteroids and aliens to increase their score. I was the sole devloper for the project. I used some assets from the unity asset store, for example, the alien spaceship and the asteroids and their textures. I programmed the gameplay mechanics. One notable feature is the damage pop-ups when the player fires and hits one of the aliens or asteroids, which in turn, decrease the visible health bars above the enemies.
